Pig Dice Game

How to Play

  • Click "Roll Dice" and roll a score between 1-6.
  • If you role a "1" your turn automatically ends and you lose your points for the round.
  • If you do not roll a "1," you may keep rolling and adding to your score.
  • Click "Hold" to end your turn and add your score for the round to your grand total.
  • The first player to get to 25 points wins.
